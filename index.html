Our Teachers




Allow us to introduce Acharya Vijay Yoga Kumar, our esteemed yoga teacher whose journey in yoga began in 1998. With a wealth of experience, Acharya Kumar served as a yoga teacher in China from 2007 to 2009. In 2010, he founded "Mind and Body Lines" in Mysore, where his innovative teaching methods, including "Reketheraphy," have transformed the lives of over 200 daily practitioners.

Acharya Kumar's expertise extends globally; he has taught over 1000 students in China. With a Diploma in Yoga Education and a string of accolades, including the title of Yoga Charya in 2012 and Yoga Bushana in 2016, Acharya Kumar embodies excellence. Under his guidance, students at Mind and Body Lines experience yoga as a holistic journey, nurturing mind, body, and spirit.
