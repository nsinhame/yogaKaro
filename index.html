Why Pranayama ?


Pranayama is the ancient yogic practice of controlled breathing, where "prana" refers to life force or vital energy, and "yama" means control. It involves various techniques to regulate and manipulate the breath for enhanced physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.

  • Stress Reduction: Pranayama techniques are renowned for their stress-relieving properties. Controlled breathing activates the parasympathetic nervous system, promoting relaxation and reducing the impact of stress on both mind and body.
  • Enhanced Focus and Concentration: Regular practice of pranayama improves oxygenation to the brain, fostering heightened mental clarity, focus, and concentration. It provides a powerful tool for sharpening the mind and improving cognitive function.
  • Balanced Energy Flow: Pranayama helps to balance the flow of prana throughout the body. By regulating the breath, practitioners can harmonize the subtle energy channels (nadis), promoting physical and energetic balance within the body.
  • Improved Respiratory Health: Through conscious control of breath, pranayama strengthens the respiratory system, increasing lung capacity and promoting healthier breathing patterns. This is particularly beneficial for individuals with respiratory conditions, promoting overall respiratory well-being.

Our Teachers




Allow us to introduce Acharya Vijay Yoga Kumar, our esteemed yoga teacher whose journey in yoga began in 1998. With a wealth of experience, Acharya Kumar served as a yoga teacher in China from 2007 to 2009. In 2010, he founded "Mind and Body Lines" in Mysore, where his innovative teaching methods, including "Reketheraphy," have transformed the lives of over 200 daily practitioners.

Acharya Kumar's expertise extends globally; he has taught over 1000 students in China. With a Diploma in Yoga Education and a string of accolades, including the title of Yoga Charya in 2012 and Yoga Bushana in 2016, Acharya Kumar embodies excellence. Under his guidance, students at Mind and Body Lines experience yoga as a holistic journey, nurturing mind, body, and spirit.
