About Us

Namaste and welcome to Mind & Body Lines, a sanctuary of serenity situated in the heart of Mysuru, India, the Yoga Capital of the World. At Mind & Body Lines, we offer more than just yoga classes; we provide a transformative experience for the mind, body, and soul. Our teachings revolve around the essential harmony of Breath, Body, and Mind, guiding practitioners toward holistic well-being and inner peace. With a dedicated team of experienced instructors, we create a supportive environment for individuals of all levels to explore the profound depths of yoga and meditation.

Beyond the mat, we foster a sense of community, organizing events, competitions, and gatherings, encouraging our students to not only enrich their personal practice but also connect with a wider network of like-minded souls. Join us on this empowering journey within, where ancient wisdom meets modern practice, and discover the boundless potential within you.
