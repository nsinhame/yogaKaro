Advanced Yoga Asanas(AYA)




Welcome to the Advanced Yoga Asanas (AYA) course at Mind & Body Lines, where we invite you to transcend the boundaries of your yoga practice under the expert guidance of our esteemed instructor, Vinay Yoga Kumar. In this transformative program, Vinay Kumar delves deep into the intricacies of advanced yoga postures, empowering practitioners to enhance their backbending, forwardbending, twisting, hand and leg balancing, and stretching abilities.

In the Advanced Yoga Asanas course, each session is thoughtfully curated to challenge and elevate your practice, encouraging you to explore the full spectrum of your physical and mental capabilities. Vinay Yoga Kumar brings a wealth of experience and a keen understanding of the body's mechanics, ensuring that every participant receives personalized attention and guidance.




This course is not just about mastering advanced poses; it's a holistic journey that integrates the physical, mental, and spiritual aspects of yoga. Vinay Yoga Kumar's teaching philosophy goes beyond the surface, fostering a deep connection with your practice and providing tools to navigate the challenges of advanced asanas. Whether you aspire to deepen your practice, refine your teaching skills, or simply experience the joy of pushing your boundaries, the Advanced Yoga Asanas course at Mind & Body Lines is designed to elevate your yoga journey to new heights.

Enroll now and embrace the transformative power of advanced yoga under the guidance of Vinay Yoga Kumar. Join us as we explore the limitless potential of the body and mind through the art of advanced yoga asanas.







Trips




At Mind & Body Lines, we believe in the transformative power of yoga not just within the confines of our studio but also in the vibrant tapestry of the world. Our Yoga Travel Adventures are curated to blend exploration, competition, and spiritual growth. Led by our dedicated team, including the seasoned instructor Vinay Yoga Kumar, we embark on journeys to sacred places like Shirdi, Belluru, Mysore, and beyond.

These yoga-centric expeditions are not just about discovering new landscapes but also about deepening our connection to the practice. Whether it's participating in renowned competitions, absorbing the spiritual energy of sacred sites, or simply immersing ourselves in the rich culture of each location, every trip is a holistic experience.




Our yoga travel experiences are open to all, fostering a sense of community among like-minded individuals. It's an opportunity to take your practice beyond the familiar, to breathe in the essence of diverse locales, and to create lasting memories with fellow yogis. Join us on these transformative journeys, where the spirit of adventure intertwines seamlessly with the philosophy of yoga, expanding horizons and enriching lives.
